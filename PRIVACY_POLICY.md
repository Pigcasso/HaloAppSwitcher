# Privacy Policy for HaloAppSwitcher

**Effective Date:** February 23, 2026

## Overview

HaloAppSwitcher is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

## Data Collection

**We collect no personal data.** The app operates entirely on your device and does not communicate with external servers.

## Local Storage

The app stores minimal settings locally on your device:
- Hotkey preferences
- Launch at login preference

This data never leaves your device.

## System Access

To function properly, the app:
- Reads the list of running applications (to display them in the switcher)
- Monitors keyboard modifier key states (to detect hotkey activation)

No keystroke data or application usage patterns are recorded or transmitted.

## Third-Party Services

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted at this URL.
