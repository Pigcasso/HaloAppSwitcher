# HaloAppSwitcher Support

Welcome to HaloAppSwitcher support! This page will help you get started and find answers to common questions.

## Quick Start

1. **Activate the Switcher**: Double-tap the Command (⌘) key
2. **Navigate**: Move your mouse to highlight apps
3. **Switch**: Click on an app icon to switch to it
4. **Close**: Click outside the ring to dismiss

## Preferences

Access preferences from the menu bar icon:
- Enable/disable launch at login
- Customize activation hotkey

## Frequently Asked Questions

### How do I activate the app switcher?
Double-tap the Command (⌘) key quickly. The ring menu will appear showing all running apps.

### Does this app require accessibility permissions?
No! HaloAppSwitcher works without any special permissions, protecting your privacy.

### Can I change the hotkey?
Currently, the app uses double-tap Command (⌘⌘) as the activation method. More hotkey options may be added in future updates.

### The switcher doesn't appear when I double-tap Command
Try adjusting your double-tap speed. Tap the Command key twice quickly (within about 0.3 seconds).

### How do I uninstall the app?
Simply drag HaloAppSwitcher from Applications to Trash. All settings will be removed automatically.
